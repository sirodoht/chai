Masala Chai with Plant Milk

Go to cow milk version
Choose number of cups to make
Cups: 2
Ingredients
    Scaling rules
    • Base recipe = 2 cups
    • Each extra cup:
      • 200 ml water
      • 1 tsp chai
      • 1 tsp sugar
      • 100 ml plant milk
    Steps
    1
    • Add 500 ml water to a pot
    • With 3 heaped tsp masala chai
    • And fresh ginger slices
    2
    • Bring to a boil
    • Simmer for 10 min
    10:00
    3
    • Add 3 tsp sugar
    • Simmer for 5 min
    5:00
    4
    • Add 250 ml plant milk
    5
    • Stir until warm
    • Do not boil
    6
    • Strain through a mesh strainer
    • Discard solids
    • Serve
