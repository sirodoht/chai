MASALA CHAI

with PLANT MILK
1

Combine in a pot:

  • 500ml water
  • Slices of fresh ginger
  • 3 heaped tsp chai
2
  • Bring to a boil
  • Then let simmer vigorously for 10 min
3
  • Add 3 tsp granulated white sugar
  • Continue simmering for 5 more minutes
  • Stir occasionally
4
  • Reduce heat to low
  • Add 250ml plant milk
5
  • Stir gently until the mixture is warm
  • Do not boil
6
  • Strain through a fine mesh sieve
  • Discard the solids
  • Serve immediately
